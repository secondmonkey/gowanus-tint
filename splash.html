The Gowanus is a canal of many colors.

Hue Gowanus tracks its moods.

I'm a Frogg, so I walk over the canal almost every day. Some days it's antifreeze-green. Other days it's fog-gray. This little project was inspired by that observation.

I'm using PIL to determine the average colors of photos of the canal taken straight-down from the Carroll Street Bridge and rendering them onto a Mapbox map.
